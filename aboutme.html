Dance

Dancing is one of my faovrite hobbies. I have been dancing for the past 10 years, 9 years in a competitve studio.
I compete and perform. Being in dance for so many years have introduced me to new people who share a passion.
I am a part of two dance teams, one at a separate studio and one at school. With my separate studio I travel and compete around the country.
Dance helps me escape my mind and into movement and music; this is one of my top ways to help reduce my stress.
It's my save space, a non-judgmental area where I can let myself go and get lost in the music. It sounds pretty basic but it is true.
